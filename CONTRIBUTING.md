Thank you for your interest in the XETO VSCode Extension!

- Why it would be valuable

Before opening a new issue, please [search existing issues](https://github.com/xetobase/xeto-vscode-extension/issues) to avoid duplicates.


## Questions?

For questions about using the extension, please:
1. Check the [README](README.md) for documentation
2. Search [existing issues](https://github.com/xetobase/xeto-vscode-extension/issues)
3. Open a new issue if your question isn't answered
